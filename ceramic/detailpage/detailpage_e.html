Description

Description Text A striking luxury personalised pet tag with engraved bone detail, perfect for keeping tabs on your furry friends! Each pet ID tag is the ultimate of all pet accessories.

Pets are part of the family, and so why not spoil your pampered pooch or cherished kitten with a bespoke engraved pet tag, to ensure the world (and it's dog!) knows who your furry friend belongs to.

Include an additional two lines of engraving on the reverse of your pet tag, to display your pet's name or additional phone number!

Choose the shape of your personalised pet tag from either a circle, heptagon or square. Our wooden pet tags are available in either small, medium or large, so be sure to select the right sized tag for your own pet's collar.

**Please note that in the UK, it is a legal requirement for your dog's pet tag to include the surname and address (specifically the door number and postcode) of the owner. It is reccomended that the dog's name is not included on the tag, but if the dog is chipped, that this is mentioned within the engraving.**

Made from genuine walnut wood, and fastened with a split ring. Dimensions are as follows:

Square: Small tag 20mm x 20mm, Medium tag 25mm x 25mm, Large tag 30mm x 30mm.

Heptagon: Small tag 20mm diameter, Medium tag 25mm diameter, Large tag 30mm diameter.

Circle: Small tag 20mm x 20mm, Medium tag 25mm x 25mm, Large tag 30mm x 30mm.

______________ How to Order _____________

Choose the size and shape of pet tag you require from the drop down menus, and add the item to your cart. In the 'Notes to Clouds and Currents' box please include the following information:

1. Wording for front engraving (15 characters maximum) (required)

2. First line of wording for reverse engraving (15 characters maximum) (if required)

3. Second line of wording for reverse engraving (15 characters maximum) (if required)
